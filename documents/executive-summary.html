Where EV charging becomes part of your journey
December 2025
Confidential

The Opportunity

Rangeway is building America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network. We guarantee climate-controlled indoor comfort at every location, an industry first.

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. Hospitality isn't something you bolt on. It's foundational to how we design every touchpoint.

The Problem

  • Drivers wait in vehicles during extreme weather
  • 70-80% industry uptime leaves drivers stranded
  • No quality restrooms or comfortable waiting areas
  • Wildly inconsistent experiences across networks

Our Solution

Two formats, one standard:

Basecamp

10-12 chargers, 2,500+ sq ft lounge, staffed cafe, premium restrooms

Waystation

8-10 chargers, 800-1,000 sq ft lounge, automated retail, same comfort guarantee

Competitive Advantage

  • Indoor Comfort Guarantee: Climate-controlled Driver's Lounge at every location. Categorical differentiator competitors cannot match.
  • Experience Consistency: Same standard at all sites. Competitors vary between parking lots and flagship lounges.
  • Pathfinder Rewards: Hospitality loyalty program. Free to join, status earned, credits back on future charging.
  • Build Right First Time: Complete from day one. No retrofit technical debt.

Business Model

  • Charging fees at $0.50/kWh base rate
  • Cafe and retail sales at Basecamps
  • Digital advertising from tourism and brand partners
  • LCFS credits in California
  • Pathfinder partner revenue and affiliates
