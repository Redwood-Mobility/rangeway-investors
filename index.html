The Investment Thesis

Why Rangeway

Untapped Market Opportunity

America's scenic highways represent a vastly underserved market for premium EV charging infrastructure. We're establishing first-mover advantage in a $3-9B market opportunity by 2030, targeting the millions of travelers who seek reliable charging beyond major interstates.

Premium Positioning

Our hospitality-first approach creates destinations, not mere charging stops. Climate-controlled sanctuaries, artisan cafes, and curated local partnerships command premium pricing while cultivating exceptional guest loyalty and repeat visits.

Defensible Network Strategy

We're strategically building along America's most iconic routes, securing premium locations at gateways to national parks and scenic destinations. Our first-mover advantage in these corridors creates lasting competitive barriers.

Mission-Aligned Team

Our team combines deep expertise in energy infrastructure, technology, and hospitality. United by a shared mission to make sustainable travel both accessible and exceptional, we're building for the long term.
