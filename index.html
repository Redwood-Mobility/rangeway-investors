Why Rangeway

Premium hospitality. Reliable infrastructure.

Premium Market, Underserved

DC fast charging is scaling fast, but the premium segment is wide open. Rangeway is establishing first-mover advantage with travelers whose vehicles already cost more than the charging experience matches.

Indoor Comfort Guarantee

Climate-controlled driver's lounge at every Waystation and Basecamp. A categorical differentiator competitors cannot match without rebuilding their networks. Inspired by Under Canvas and AutoCamp.

Per-Project Capital Strategy

Each site is capitalized on its own merits with the partners and instruments appropriate to that location. Phased development at Mojave validates the model before scaling.

Hospitality DNA

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. Our team and advisors combine deep hospitality experience with EV charging, infrastructure finance, and energy systems expertise.
