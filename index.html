Investment Thesis

Why Rangeway

Untapped Market Opportunity

DC fast charging is scaling from $1.0B to $26.2B by 2035, with 18,000 new DC ports added in the US in 2025 alone. Rangeway is establishing first-mover advantage in the premium segment, targeting travelers who expect their charging experience to match the vehicles they drive.

Premium Positioning

Our hospitality-first approach creates destinations, not mere charging stops. Climate-controlled sanctuaries, artisan cafes, and curated local partnerships command premium pricing while cultivating exceptional guest loyalty and repeat visits.

Defensible Network Strategy

We're strategically securing prime corridor locations before competitors recognize the opportunity. A four-format network (Trailhead, Waystation, Basecamp, Summit) lets us match each site to its market while creating lasting competitive barriers through real estate control.

Mission-Aligned Team

Our team combines deep expertise in energy infrastructure, technology, and hospitality. United by a shared mission to make sustainable travel both accessible and exceptional, we're building for the long term.
